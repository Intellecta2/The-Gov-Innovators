Avyay

A mesh-based safety network ensuring women's distress alerts reach help without connectivity.

See The Problem

The Problem

Isolated Areas

Women face threats in isolated or dimly lit areas with no one around to help.

Critical Barriers

Small barriers like weak signal, no internet, or panic can decide life or death.

Existing Apps Fail

Most safety apps fail as they rely on a stable internet connection and visible actions.

Help is Too Late

Tragedies happen not from a lack of help, but because it doesn't arrive in time.

Our Unique Solution

  • Works 100% Offline

    Alerts are sent via a smart mesh-network of nearby phones using Bluetooth & Wi-Fi Direct. No internet or mobile signal required.

  • Human-Powered Response

    Instantly notifies a network of nearby verified responders, trusted contacts, and police for the fastest possible help.

  • Silent & Secure

    Features silent activation (e.g., power button) and a "Dummy-PIN" mode to protect the user without alerting the attacker.

A Scalable Business Model

B2C Freemium

Core offline alert features are free to maximize network growth. Premium features (like Dummy-PIN & AI summaries) are a recurring subscription.

B2B Subscriptions

We sell "Avyay-Safe Campus" packages to universities, corporate offices, and residential societies, providing them a private dashboard and responder network.

Our Technology

Mobile: React Native (iOS & Android)
Offline Network: BLE & Wi-Fi Direct Mesh (react-native-ble-plx)
On-Device AI: TensorFlow Lite (Local danger scoring, AI summary)
Backend & Security: Node.js, AWS (Lambda, SQS), & AES-256 Encryption

Challenges & Mitigation

Challenge Mitigation
Offline Communication Use robust libraries (BLE Plx, Nearby Connections) & test on common device models.
Security & Privacy Risk Mandatory user verification (e.g., Aadhaar/DigiLocker) for responders. Encrypt all alert data.
Police Integration MVP will use webhooks/email alerts to existing systems. Full API integration is a long-term goal.
Battery & Background OS Use battery-efficient foreground services & optimize scanning intervals to minimize drain.
